# ğŸ“– [RECIPE-APP] <a name="about-project"></a>

The Recipe app is designed to help you organize your recipes, ingredients, and pantry. With this app, you can save ingredients, manage your inventory, create new recipes, and generate shopping lists based on your inventory and recipe needs. You can also choose to share your recipes publicly, making them accessible to anyone.
